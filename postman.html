GDS to Postman / Mail Guard Promotion

This page provides eligibility, exam pattern, syllabus, and study material details for GDS promotion to Postman / Mail Guard.

Eligibility

  • Minimum 3 years of continuous service as GDS
  • Should meet departmental eligibility conditions
  • Must qualify departmental competitive examination

Exam Pattern

  • Written Examination (Objective Type)
  • Total Marks: 150
  • Duration: 120 minutes
  • No negative marking

Syllabus Overview

  • General Knowledge
  • Basic Mathematics
  • Reasoning
  • Postal Rules & Procedures
